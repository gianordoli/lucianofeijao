Luciano Feijão

  • Work
  • About

Luciano Feijão is an educator, artist and illustrator born in Vitória, Brazil. His latest exhibits examine racial-ethnic themes, portraying the black population in opposition to the processes of stigmatization and annihilation it has been subjected to.

His work is characterized by its gestural expressiveness, the density of its shapes and the use of alternative drawing tools.

His recent field of research, titled Antianatomy, explores the racist biases in pseudo-scientific policies.
